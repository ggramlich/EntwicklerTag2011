Define a team roster for Dallas

|Team  |Dallas              |
|number|last name|first name|
|0     |Marion   |Shawn     |
|41    |Nowitzki |Dirk      |


The players are now assigned to their shirt numbers and we can verify their names.
If a shirt number or team is not defined, the player is name "unknown".

![ script
Player number 41 of Dallas is Dirk Nowitzki
Player number 23 of Dallas is unknown
Player number 41 of XYZ is unknown
]!
