Define a team roster for Germany

|Team  |Germany             |
|number|last name|first name|
|5     |Herber   |Johannes  |
|14    |Nowitzki |Dirk      |

The players are now assigned to their shirt numbers and we can verify their names.
If a shirt number or team is not defined, the player is name "unknown".

![ script
Player number 14 of Germany is Dirk Nowitzki
Player number 23 of Germany is unknown
Player number 14 of XYZ is unknown
]!
